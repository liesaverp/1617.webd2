Art concepts

We can offer three different styles of custom artwork, depending on your preferences and budget. Please browse our portfolio for some great examples of each category.

Cartoon / Simplistic

Crisp linework, straightforward shading. Ideal for quick concepts and smaller budgets. Simply mail us a description or reference picture(s) of your character(s).

Prices:
$12 for any bust ($10 for each extra bust).
$30 for single-character fullbody drawings (extra characters within one drawing are $20 each).

Semi-realism

Want to kick it up a notch?
Perhaps you will find our semi-realism commission category more favourable. We deliver high-resolution, high-quality digital art, adjusted to your specific needs, no matter what! With each order, you receive two files: one for printing (pdf) and one for online usage (png).

Prices:
$25 for any bust ($20 for each extra bust).
$75 for single-character fullbody drawings (extra characters within one drawing are $50 each).
$100 for any book cover, no matter the complexity. Publication & distribution rights included.

Traditional

Not a fan of digital art? We also offer a wide arrange of traditional work, shipped to your doorstep! Products are made on sturdy paper or durable canvas and come with a smear-proof protective coat.

Prices:
$12 for A4 bust ($10 for each extra bust; shipping not included).
$30 for A4 single-character fullbody drawings (extra characters are $20 each; shipping not included).
