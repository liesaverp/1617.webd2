Meet the team

Matthias Story

I am Matthias (also known as Captain Ghostly, lovingly nicknamed El Capitan by Liesa) and I love technology and creativity. I spend my free time programming, creating games, crafting cosplays, drawing and watching movies or series. I also like to hang out and chill with my friends!

What we do

We are a small business, consisting of two students, aiming to bring affordable products to small businesses and individuals. We work in an amicable, open atmosphere, while keeping a close eye on quality.

We work hard at achieving our scholar degrees, and this small business can help facilitate that: we improve our skills through experience and take some of the financial strain off our parents. (Some school expenses can be high in cost).
